About Me

I'm Aung Kaung Myat aka RETO, a passionate and creative graphic designer/front end dev based in Yangon. I specialize in creating visually compelling designs that enhance user engagement and deliver impactful messages. With experience in crafting YouTube thumbnails and in-game visuals, I've developed a strong eye for detail and a deep understanding of design principles.

My journey in graphic design started during my time at ConceptX International School, where I first discovered my love for bringing ideas to life through visual art. Since then, I've worked on projects, where I've had the opportunity to hone my skills and contribute to exciting digital content.

I'm always looking for new challenges and opportunities to grow as a designer. Whether it's developing innovative designs for a brand or enhancing the visual experience of a game, I'm dedicated to producing high-quality work that resonates with audiences.
